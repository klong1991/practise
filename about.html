If you survived war, what would you do if you found out life had other plans for you? So Long and Goodnight follows one man, who returns to the only place he feels at home after discovering his time is running out. As his time nears the end, he reflects on those he's left behind, while considering what's happening now - and what comes next?

Set to an incredible orcestration of the great hits of My Chemical Romance. Experience "Sleep" "The Light Behind Your Eyes" "Our Lady of Sorrows","The Ghost of You","Helena (So Long and Goodnight)" and many more, as you've never experienced them before.
